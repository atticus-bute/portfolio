    Organizers

    Belleville Running Club

    The Belleville Running Club is a running group based out of Belleville, Illinois, USA. Our group meets on a regular basis in or around Belleville, IL.

    St. Clair County Bar Association

    The St. Clair County Bar Association is a private, nonprofit organization that offers services to members of the St. Clair County legal profession and to the local general public.
